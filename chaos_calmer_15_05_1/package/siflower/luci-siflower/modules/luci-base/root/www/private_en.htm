Statement on Siflower Intelligent Router and Privacy

Siflower Intelligent Router is a product that provides you with home intelligent routing service operated by Qingdao Siflower Communication Planning Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "Siflower"). We understand the importance of personal information to you and will do our utmost to protect your personal information. We are committed to protecting your personal information with appropriate security measures in accordance with industry-improved security standards.

Please read and understand this Statement on Siflower Smart Router and Privacy before using our products.

You promise to be an adult when you start using this product/service. If you are a minor, your guardian is required to agree to your use of the app and agree to the applicable terms of application.

1. How do we collect and use your personal information?

Personal information refers to various information recorded electronically or otherwise that can identify a specific natural person or reflect the activity of a particular natural person, either alone or in combination with other information.

Personal sensitive information refers to the identity card number, personal biometric information, bank account number, property information, whereabouts, transaction information, personal information of children under the age of 14 (including).

We collect and use your personal information only for the purposes described in this statement:

(1) Provide you with the following core functional services

1. Business function 1: Online upgrade

In order to implement the online upgrade function, we need to collect the following information: software version number, device model, device identifier. The purpose of collecting the software version number and device model is to obtain whether the device has a new version that can be upgraded. This feature uploads this information to the server, queries the new version, and returns the results of the new version. The purpose of collecting device identifiers is primarily to count user data.

We will use your personal information in the following manner: Only the device identifier in the above data will be stored on the Siflower server for a period of 6 months; other data will not be stored.

If you do not provide this type of information, your device will not be eligible for the online upgrade service.

2, business function two: smart home gateway

In order to implement the smart home gateway function, we need to collect the following information: router device information (device identifier, MAC address, devID), smart home device information (name, MAC address, vendor, device identifier, devID, software version number) ). The router device information is collected so that the router can register the server and the user can manage the router device. The name, MAC address, and manufacturer of the smart home device are collected for the purpose of sensing the state of the smart home device. The brand, device identifier, devID, and software version number of the smart home device are collected in order to register the smart home device with the server.

We will use your personal information in the following ways: transmitted and encrypted stored on the Siflower server. After you unbind your device from your account, your personal data saved on the server will be deleted immediately.

If you do not provide this type of information, you will not be able to use the Smart Home Gateway feature.

3. Business function three: remote device management

In order to implement remote device management functions, we need to collect the following information: router device information (device identifier, MAC address, devID) and the account of the device that is bound to the device. Collecting router device information and Siflower account information is for the router to register with the server, the user can manage the router device.

We will use your personal information in the following ways: transmitted and encrypted stored on the Siflower server. After you unbind your device from your account, your personal data saved on the server will be deleted immediately.

If you do not provide this type of information, you can choose to unbind the Changchang account from the router device and you will not be able to remotely manage your router device.

4. Business function four: user experience improvement plan

To implement the User Experience Improvement Program, we need to collect the following information: router device information (device identifier, software version number), LAN device information (device name, device type, brand, vendor), error log. Collecting router device information, LAN device information, and function usage data is used to calculate the status of the home network and the usage status of the corresponding function usage frequency, which provides a basis for us to enhance the user experience later. Collecting error log information is used to locate related faults when the device is operating abnormally.

We will use your personal information in the following ways: transmitted and encrypted stored on the Siflower server. The shelf life is 6 months.

If you do not provide this type of information, you can choose not to join the User Experience Improvement Program, your router device will not be able to quickly improve the user experience, and we will not be able to quickly resolve your router's abnormalities and failures.

2. Provide your personal information to third party service providers

This product does not provide your personal information to third parties other than Siflower.
