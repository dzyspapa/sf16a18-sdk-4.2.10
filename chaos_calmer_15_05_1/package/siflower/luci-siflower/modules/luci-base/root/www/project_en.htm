User Experience Improvement Program

Thank you for your use and love for the products of Siflower! Before you participate in the Siflower Product User Experience Improvement Program, please be sure to read and understand this policy carefully. You have the right to choose not to participate in the user experience improvement program of the product, but once you start to participate, your behavior will be regarded as an understanding and consent to the full content of this policy.

The user understands and agrees to the following policies of the Siflower Product User Experience Improvement Program:

1. The User Experience Improvement Program collects device version information, application statistics, behavior data and error log data of users using Siflower products, and generates a small amount of data traffic.

2. The above data will be anonymized after being collected and encrypted and sent to the Siflower server. After statistics and analysis, Siflower will be used to discover improvements in products and services and enhance the user experience. In the process, the above data collected and processed cannot be used to identify an individual.

3. The above data may be used to enhance the personalized experience of the user experience and services. In addition, in order to help Siflower's partners and third-party developers to improve their applications, products and services for Siflower products, Siflower may provide some summary statistics and diagnostic data related to their applications or services. At this point, the responsibility to protect your data will be borne by them. In addition, Siflower may disclose the above data at the request of the law or government departments.

4. Users can improve by setting up or not participating in the experience at any time. If you choose not to participate in user experience improvements, Siflower will stop collecting the above data.

5. The above data will be kept in the server of Siflower. Siflower will not disclose and disseminate the above data, nor will it use the data for other non-Changchang products.

6. The above data will be retained for a period of time at Siflower Server and will be destroyed after completion of statistical analysis in Siflower.

7. Siflower does not assume any responsibility for personal data collected or disclosed by third parties other than Siflower products through applications or programs other than Siflower products.
